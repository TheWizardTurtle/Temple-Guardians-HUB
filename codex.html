Temple Guardian Codex

Introduction

The Temple Guardians are an elite division within the New Jedi Order, known for their exceptional command of both combat and the law. Their understanding of Jedi legal codes is unmatched, making them the foremost authority in upholding order within the Temple.

Skilled and disciplined, Temple Guardians are fully capable of defending against any threat, including raiders and intruders, with precision and strength.

Regulations

Outfit Regulations

  • Pledge - Temple Guardian: Must wear the preset TG robes
  • Senior Temple Guardian - Captain: May use personalized TG robes (must resemble TG)
  • Commander+: Any attire of their choice

Discharging

Discharge requires 2-week wait & Primary Training. Unauthorized exit = 90-day blacklist. Format is pinned in Discharge Channel.

On-Duty Protocol

  • Use Guardian hilt unless higher rank
  • Lightsaber always in hand
  • Juyo in formation, preferred style in combat
  • TK ON at all times
  • AFK only 15min in Dojo
  • No emotes, holocoms, or AFK outside dojo
  • STG+ assist academy

Off-Duty Behavior

  • Remain professional
  • Join TG to defend if raider joins
  • Dojo is on-duty only

Oath of Silence

  • Active when leaving dojo
  • Monitors/LT whisper only if needed
  • Captains+ may speak freely
  • Only Captains+ can disable [OOS]

Line Regulations

  • Formation 2 blocks from line
  • HR right, LR left
  • KOS if raider crosses
  • No solo duels unless only one TG is on

Main Group

  • Training certificate needed to host
  • Only TG+ may get padawans
  • Close training if hostile joins
  • Follow basic regs, log warnings, keep dojo door closed
  • Commands from HR+ and JHC+ are to be followed

Academy

The TG Academy is now a core internal system. All STG+ must participate by hosting or mentoring.

  • 2 Academy Phases = 1 TG Training (for quota)
  • Phase 4 only hosted by Monitor+
