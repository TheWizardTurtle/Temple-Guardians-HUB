Lieutenant ➤ Captain

Information & Pathway Overview

Promotion Method:
This is a handpicked rank, determined by the Chief of Security and the Temple Guardian Commander. There is no checklist or formal application.
Expectations:
As a Captain, you are entrusted with leadership over a TG Sub-Division (e.g. Honor Guard or Academy). You are expected to uphold the highest standards of integrity, law, and leadership.
Significance:
Captains are key figures in shaping the Temple Guardian future. You are recognized as a role model, a strategic leader, and a guardian of our values and legacy.
